Hello everyone! My name is Mary and I am a web developer. I find passion in problem solving and in seeing new designs come to life. I'm a biology and chemistry graduate who realized after many years in the lab that coding is my true passion.


So I took the leap and started the Front End Web Developer bootcamp at Thinkful/Bloc. Recently I enrolled in Launch School - a mastery based program on the track to becoming a Software Engineer. I have always been someone who is constantly learning, so I jumped at the opportunity to learn development. I am a person who is incredibly self-disciplined and always looks for a solution when things get difficult. A few fun facts about myself: I live in Chicago. I love craft beers, and when I'm not working on a website or new app you can find me outdoors or reading my latest book club pick.
